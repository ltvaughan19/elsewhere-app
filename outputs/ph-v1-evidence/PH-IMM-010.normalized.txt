BI e-Services — Bureau of Immigration

Welcome to Bureau of Immigration Online Services
Manage your immigration needs in one convenient dashboard
All Systems Operational

Listed services (landing page):
- ANNUAL REPORT — Submit annual reports for all registered aliens and ACR I-Card holders (except tourists).
- CRUISE VISA WAIVER — Request visa waiver for cruise passengers from visa-required countries.
- ECC-B/RP/SRC WITH AR COMPLIANCE — For departing immigrants/non-immigrants with valid ACR I-Cards, including Annual Report compliance.
- EMIGRATION CLEARANCE CERTIFICATE - B — Secure exit clearance for ACR I-Card holders temporarily leaving the Philippines.
- EXISTING SCHOOL REGISTRATION — Register previously accredited schools not yet onboard with BI's online system.
- RETAIN/RE-ACQUIRE PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP — Apply to retain or re-acquire citizenship for naturalized Filipinos.
- SPECIAL STUDY PERMIT — For foreign nationals below 18 or those taking non-degree courses under 1 year.
- STUDENT VISA CONVERSION (9F) — For foreign nationals 18+ taking higher education in accredited institutions.
- TOURIST VISA EXTENSION — Extend your tourist visa quickly and conveniently online.
- VISA WAIVER — Extend your 30-day visa-free stay with a 29-day visa waiver.
- WAIVER OF EXCLUSION GROUND — Apply for unaccompanied minors under 15 to enter the Philippines legally.

Important Notice: You need to login first to access this service.
